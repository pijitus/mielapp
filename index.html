Years of Expertise

Family Tradition, excellence in honey

Rossini Family is a beekeeping unit that has been operating under Good Apicultural Practices (BPL) for more than 30 years, knowing the national and international requirements of the product, which is monofloral carob honey. In addition to this honey, during different stages, other honeys are also obtained, such as: sunflower, wild forest (monte), calden, and molle, in different proportions depending on the year.

During part of the year, the base is Lincoln, where the material is conditioned and all complementary activities are carried out that allow us to begin the high season during the first days of August.

Logistics and planning are carried out in Lincoln because that is where everything starts and it is the firm's base city.

The objective of achieving a monofloral honey leads us first to the province of La Pampa, where all our hives spend the winter in the city of Maisonave and its areas of influence; once the hive is fed, it is moved to the province of Mendoza in the area of Real del Padre. This allows the hives that have passed the winter to increase their population in order to have maximum productivity.

All apiaries are permanently monitored by highly trained personnel with more than 30 years of experience in the sector. Any anomaly is detected immediately, which allows us to take immediate corrective action.

Once our hives have an adequate population and the nucleation work is carried out in Mendoza, a large part of the productive mass sets course for the next transhumance that takes us to the province of Cordoba, and there the carob flowering starts, which is our main objective.

All hives are distributed in fields previously prepared and suitable for the activity.

After the flowering ends in the province of Cordoba, where contact is made with the first honey of the season, we return to the province of Mendoza pursuing our objective of carob honey, because in these latitudes the flowering is delayed; this allows us to go in search of honey with these characteristics once again.

The planning and organizational capacity of our firm to achieve a honey with these characteristics is noteworthy.

Extraction Room

Since the end of 2024, the company has had an extraction room authorized for this activity.

It is a room with all its machinery made of stainless steel, complying with both national and international legislation.

Our goal and what the company aims for is to be able to transcend borders and bring our product to consumers who request it; being able to export our product to other countries is our objective.

For this, season after season, we invest in and train each of the members of our company.
